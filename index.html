Time of Day

A JavaScript Project by Andrei Novozhilov

Greeting!

JavaScript determines the current hour, and presents a different image, custom message, and color based on each of the following times of day:
Morning (5am to 12 noon)
Afternoon (12 noon to 6pm)
Evening (6pm to 11pm)
Night (11pm to 5am)
Each picture is a photo of me at different times of the day.
